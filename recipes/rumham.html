Rumham

    Ingredients

  • 1 dinner ham
  • 3 parts your choice of spiced rum
  • 1 part pineapple juice
  • 1 part brown sugar
  • Salt
  • Water
  • Pineapple slices and maraschino cherries

    Steps

  1. Combine rim and pineapple juice in stockpot
  2. Add ham to pot
  3. Add enough water to cover the ham
  4. Let ham sit overnight (upto 24 hours)
  5. Oven should be preheated 45 minutes before serving
  6. Place stockpot with ham in oven at 200 degrees
  7. Let simmer till ham is warmed through
  8. Garnish with pineapple slices and cherry
  9. Serve, preferably on a life-raft floating out to sea

Milksteak

    ingredients

  • 1 cup whole milk
  • 1 sixteen ounce filet mignion
  • 1 cup jellybeans, raw
  • 1/2 cup sweetened condensed milk
  • 1/2 teaspoon cornstarch

    Steps

  1. mix all milk ingredients with the cornstarch on a pot at low heat
  2. when reaches 200f put steak into concotion
  3. take steak out after 15 minutes
  4. garnish with milksauce and jellybeans
  5. serve

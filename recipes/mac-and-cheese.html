Mac and Cheese

Ingredients

  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 1/2 cups water
  • 1/2 cup panko breadcrumbs
  • 3-4 tbsp butter
  • 8 ounces pastsa
  • 2 ounces romano cheese
  • 4 ounces american cheddar
  • cayanne pepper

Steps

  1. combine milk and water, bring to simmer
  2. in seperate pan, combine breadcrumbs and butter
  3. when milk simmers, add pasta
  4. toss golden brown breadcrumbs with 2 ounces grated cheese
  5. when liquid is absorbed add american cheese and stir
  6. add shake of cayanne
  7. top with breadcrumbs after plating in bowl and serve!
